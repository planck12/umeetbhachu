# Umeet Bhachu, P.Eng., PMP, CMRP

**Rotating Equipment Engineer | Turbomachinery | Reliability & Advanced Analytics**

Umeet Bhachu is a professional Rotating Equipment Engineer with over 20 years of experience in oil & gas, LNG, and energy infrastructure. His expertise includes pumps, compressors, gas turbines, mechanical seals, lubrication systems, and reliability engineering.

He has authored technical articles in recognized industry publications including *Hydrocarbon Engineering* and *LNG Industry*, and his publications are indexed on Google Scholar.

## Research & Technical Interests
- Rotating equipment reliability
- Turbomachinery performance monitoring
- Nonlinear causality & transfer entropy
- Machine learning for industrial analytics
- Asset performance optimization
